Formula 1 Teams


RedBull
The Redbull team has been dominant in the sport for years, led by their head drivers Max Verstappen and Sergio Perez. They are based in Milton Keynes, UK and have been in the sport since 1997. They have 4 world championships titles and look to capture a fifth one this season. Their team chief is Christian Horner.
Mercedes
Mercedes is another force to be reckoned with in the formula 1 league. They frequently bud heads with the Redbull team for the team championship. Their drivers include Lewis Hamilton and George Russell. The former being a seven time world champion. They have been in the sport since 1970 and their team chief is the infamous Toto Wolf.
Ferrari
Ferrari is a well known car maker but even more in the world of formula 1. They have been around since the inception of formula 1 and is known for its decorated history of wins and championships. They are always in the mix and a force to be reckon with. They are led by drivers Charles Leclerc and Carlos Sainz. They have amassed 16 world championships in its history. They are led by team chief Mattia Binotto.
McLaren
Mclaren are also veterans in the sport since their inception in 1966. They have won a total of 8 world championships and are led by drivers Daniel Ricciardo and Lando Norris. Their team chief is newcomer Andreas Seidl and their objective this season is stay in the mix and bein in as many points as possible.
